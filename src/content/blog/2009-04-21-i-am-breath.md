I am breathing I am within I am sitting, softly upon the earth I am eyes closed I am breathing I am expansive and listening I am not alone I am together I am wind on my face call of the robin grass on my legs I am unseen clouds over unknown lands I am waters to the ends of thought laughter and sadness love and apathy I am universal I am without I am compassion I am breath
