Break free the coarse rope  
Untether this being from the base  
Mount the steed and find thy drum  
Tonight the fey offer you hope

  
No sword or shield can protect now  
Incantation and spell do weave  
Book and scroll burn cold to touch  
Tonight the fey remind you how
