"My father is the pure awareness of rigpa, Samantabhadra, My mother, the space of all things, Samantabhadri, My line, the indivisibility of awareness and space, My name, the glorious Lotus Born, My homeland, the unborn dharmadhatu, My sustenance, consuming dualistic thoughts, My destiny, to accomplish the actions of the buddhas of past, present and future."[![12027609_951065374960388_8730218886153165781_n](https://www.hologramthoughts.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/12027609_951065374960388_8730218886153165781_n.jpg)](https://www.hologramthoughts.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/12027609_951065374960388_8730218886153165781_n.jpg)

Padmasambhava, also known as Guru Rinpoche, the ‘Precious Master’, is the founder of Tibetan Buddhism and considered to be the Buddha of our time.
