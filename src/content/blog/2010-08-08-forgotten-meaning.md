What if... What if an entire planet had lost the meaning of their purpose? What if the whole race had fallen asleep and awoke without knowing what their duty was? What if millennia ago the planet Earth was one of many planets in communication with each other, and the people who lived then knew from where they came, and also knew that one day we would be reunited with others like ourselves? What if the ruling elite of this planet have always known, and hidden the truth from the common man? What if they do so to protect themselves, and to deny the truth that would set us all free? What if we opened our eyes and realized that we can have a world based upon abundance, not greed? That we can feed everyone, clothe everyone, shelter everyone? The only people that would suffer are those that rule by fear mongering and malice. Open your eyes and see how valuable you are.
